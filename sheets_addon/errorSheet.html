Something feel broken? This can occur due to AirDNA not having sufficient data on the address you've specified.

A few scenarios can cause this:
  • AirDNA doesn't have enough data on that address's region.
    • Search on AirDNA to find nearby neighborhoods/regions/cities with enough data.
    • For example, 'Els Hostalets de Pierola, Barcelona' has no data, but nearby 'Esparreguera' does.
  • The property you are appraising has more bedrooms than other properties in the surrounding area.
    • If the property has more than 5 bedrooms, try lowering the number of bedrooms one-at-a-time and rerunning the appraisal.
  • We might be having difficulty getting the city or region ID from our 3rd party sources.
    • We use this ID to get percentile data. If none of the above tips works, reach out to spencer@ or ashby@ for help.
